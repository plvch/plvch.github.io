Practice

Financial planning

I've been fascinated by finance as an industry for years. Moving to Germany turned that toward the household side: how a family actually plans, saves, and decides.
That became geldchen.com, a separate financial-education project.
Now I'm building a practice for individual financial planning.

It's planning, not product-picking. I don't advise on specific instruments.
We start from a conversation about your situation and map your savings, investments, and assets against your goals. You get a concrete set of documents: a clear picture of where you stand now, and the calculation toward where you want to be then.

Not open yet. The practice is still in preparation and isn't taking clients.
I'm running case research right now, so if you want to talk through your situation, reach out.

Scope. Information and planning support only. No product sales, and no advice on specific financial instruments, taxes, or legal questions.
Every decision stays yours. See the Impressum and privacy notice.
